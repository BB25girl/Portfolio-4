about.

As a Newport News native, I have always dreamed of a life beyond the so called "Bad Newz". My goal while I'm here is to teach my self the very valuable skills of web development. I'm not a complete newbie, seeing as I am a child of the MySpace era. I hope to learn skills that will land me in a career I am truly passionate about. I curently landed me a job as a Junior Graphic Designer at a home decor company here in my hometown. I am so blessed to be doing something so soon that I enjoy doing,but I know this is not the end of my journey.

Graphic design is much different than web design and I hope to in the next few years to explore more of the web design world as I continue to build on my skills. I hope one day soon to move to Richmond, VA to truly start my career as a web developer. I am a former student of VCU having majored in Fashion Merchandising, so the move is truly to get back to my second home.My end goal would to be able to land a job in my dream home of Montreal, Canada, supporting my self as a successful web developer for a high end fashion company.
